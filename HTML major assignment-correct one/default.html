Hey and Welcome to Venice↑

Welcome and thanks for visiting my website.I enjoy travelling particularly bag-packing.There is so much to see out there and tons of cultures to discover and explore so I dont miss out on an opportunity

The second education everyone should strive to have is travelling.It will change your approach to and your philosophy of life.

That's me in Venice,(pronounced 'Venetzia' in Italian) in Italy in December hence I am wearing a winter jacket,I nevertheless had a blast.

Mexico City↑

Look at me again in Mexico city with a bunch of tourists.We met eachother at the tourist hostel and hit it off and decided to visit the Mexico City Zoo,the National Anthropological Museum and got to know each other more.

Mexico as a country has a bad reputation in terms of safety and other things but I couldn't feel any safer.Mexicans are an amazing people and life there is cheap.Most of all,I got to practice my Spanish.Many tourists visit the city throughout the year because there are lots of things to do and to see.

The subway system is cheap and efficient but can be crowded and women and children use seperate wagons.

Amsterdam↑

Welcome to the city of Amsterdam or Sin city as somepeople would call it.Everything is allowed here,even things you never imagined.

Everyone rides a bicycle and I saw more bicycles here than I had ever seen in my entire life.Even professionally dressed women elderly women were riding bicycles,menaing what is taboo in one place is fine in another place.

Milan↑

Milan,the fashion capital of Europe,to be honest,there wasn't much to see with the exception a big Cathedral.If you are a fashion freak then you will gets lots of good stuff to buy because some of the biggest fashion weeks in Europe take place here.Florence may be have been a better option but Milan was pretty close to Venice so it was easier and faster to get to.
